As a cook, I want recipes matched to what I have at home so I can make dinner without shopping
As a shopper, I want a generated grocery list so I can buy what I need more easily
As a user, I want an easy-to-use interface so I can efficiently add new ingredients and recipes
As a homeowner, I want a way to add newly-bought ingredients to my pantry
As a planner, I want a list of meal parts that can be combined so I can create my own meals from pieces
As an eater, I want a way to prevent cooking the same meal too often so I won't get tired of eating it
